Our family has been making Wood for Fords for over 50 years. We have a 30 Day money back guarantee for returns on new, un-used, clean wood; customer pays return shipping. Do not alter, paint, or preserve wood until you're sure the wood fits. We guarantee that our wood will fit original, USA made Fords. The original wood was, in many instances, slightly different from car to car. The wood we send should fit a typical body, even if the new wood and your original wood differ slightly.
