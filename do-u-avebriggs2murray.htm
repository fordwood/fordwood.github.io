Do you have a Briggs or a Murray Fordor?
 

The Briggs window tops are flat and the top of the glass is flat. The Briggs door bottom closes above the sill plate, leaving the edge of the sill exposed.

  
 
 
 

 

The Murray window tops are arched and the top of the glass is arched. The Murray door bottoms close flush with the bottom of the sill plate, covering the sill edge.
